Participant Information & Consent

Welcome, and thank you for your interest in our study.

We are a team of researchers working to better understand human maturography—the patterns and indicators of human maturity across different stages of life. Our work focuses on:

  • Monitoring human maturity progress
  • Identifying and diagnosing maturity-related disorders
  • Informing decisions in selecting or appointing individuals for leadership roles
  • Conducting broader research to advance scientific knowledge about human maturity

Your participation in this online study will involve completing a set of questions about your experiences, behaviors, and decision-making patterns. There are no right or wrong answers — please respond honestly based on your personal perspective.

All information collected will remain confidential and will be used solely for research purposes in accordance with ethical standards. Participation is completely voluntary. You may exit the form at any time before submission without any consequences.

By clicking “I Agree & Continue,” you confirm that you have read and understood the information above and voluntarily consent to participate.
